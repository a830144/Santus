Sanctus

Who We Are

Sanctus is a charity providing a café, housing and development space in the heart of Chelmsford for the homeless, day-homeless, destitute and those fallen on hard times. In difficult financial times we are here to assist people who struggle for a hot meal, and to offer support to rebuild their lives.

We have been running since December 2011 from Sanctus Cafe (28 Broomfield Road). We are open 7 days a week and offer free of charge a variety of breakfast, freshly cooked lunches, drinks and snacks throughout the day to between 15 and 30 people. Since October 2013 we have been providing shelter to aid housing for the homeless alongside training and support to enable our clients to gain confidence and to get back on their feet into the community and workplace. We are developing a series of awareness raising community-based projects to lessen the stigma of homelessness and encourage individual, student and business responsibility to the community around us.

We would like to thank our partners and sponsors for their kind donations and support. If you would like to help too, please visit our page for more information.

Our mission

01

To continue being a lifeline for the homeless & hungry

To treat the homeless & those looking for more from life with great human values in order for them to rebuild their confidence, resources & in turn, we trust, their lives

02

To provide a space of sanctuary & normality where with small steps action can be taken, to generate confidence & self-respect

To build skills, development programmes & support for craft & trade opportunities

03

To evolve projects for local business support & homeless development

To raise monies & awareness throughout the community for the ongoing support of vulnerable & homeless
